I'm a full-stack software engineer and entrepreneur living in NYC, specializing in generalizing. I grew up in WI where I started programming TI calculator games in middle school, graduated Brown '09 with a degree in computer science and math, spent some time working at Pixar on the film Up and at Microsoft on a research operating system codenamed Midori, and a few years ago moved out to NYC to start the engineering side of Stamped which got acquired by Yahoo! in the Fall of 2012.

I am a passionate, experienced developer who loves to stay diverse, but my background is strongest in the following topics: computer graphics / rendering (2D / 3D, online/offline, OpenGL/DirectX, etc.), server-side scalability (AWS, MongoDB, various databases and frameworks, etc.), client-side web development (HTML5, JS, CSS, WebGL, etc.), iOS development, structured data, programming languages (Python, Scala, C++, Javascript, Objective-C, etc) and startup engineering. I also love creating interesting visualizations and generative art, much of which will be hosted on this site.

The purpose of this site is to act both as a personal portfolio as well as a blog for me to post (hopefully) helpful articles about engineering topics that interest me.
